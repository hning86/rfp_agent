# **Request for Proposal: H\&R Block Advertising Campaign**

# **1\. Executive Summary**

H\&R Block is seeking a strategic media and creative partner to develop and execute an integrated advertising campaign for the upcoming tax season. Our primary goal is to drive brand consideration and reach among our core demographic, specifically focusing on the "broad sensible optimist" audience. Building on the success of previous efforts, such as the December Refund Advance (RA) campaign and multicultural initiatives, we aim to leverage data-driven insights to maximize efficiency and conversion volume.

# **2\. Company Background & Context**

H\&R Block operates in a highly seasonal industry, with the peak period occurring between January and mid-April. Historically, the majority of the annual marketing budget is deployed during this window (e.g., $27M spent through April vs. $1M for the remainder of the year). Challenges such as government shutdowns or changes in tax law often cause consumer confusion, necessitating clear and authoritative brand messaging to guide clients through the early portion of tax season.

# **3\. Campaign Objectives**

The agency will be tasked with meeting the following performance benchmarks based on historical data:

* **Increase Brand Consideration:** Aim for a relative lift of at least 2.2%, targeting over 120k additional users.  
* **Maximize Reach:** Achieve a unique reach of 11M+ users across digital platforms.  
* **Drive Efficiency:** Target a Cost Per Lifted User (CPLU) of approximately $0.81 and aim for a Retail CPA that is at least 24% lower for specialized multicultural segments.  
* **Search Lift:** Drive an increase in brand keyword searches (historical benchmark: 69%).

# **4\. Target Audience**

Proposals should specifically address how to reach and engage the following segments:

* **The "Sensible Optimist":** Broad audience across all products.  
* **Key Demographics:**  
  * Age group 35-44 (historically shown 7.9% relative lift).  
  * Working Class Savers (historically shown 8.9% relative lift).  
  * Male audiences.  
* **Multicultural (USM):** Capturing relevant intent online at efficient rates.  
* **Mobile-First Users:** 91% of our on-target reach is typically achieved on mobile and tablet devices.

# **5\. Scope of Work & Deliverables**

The selected agency will be responsible for:

## **Strategic Planning**

* Developing a multi-funnel approach utilizing YouTube (TrueView for Action, Bumper ads).  
* Implementing "Target CPA" (tCPA) bidding strategies to optimize conversion volume.  
* Utilizing Brand & Generic Custom Intent audiences to target users explicitly searching for tax preparation products.

## **Creative Development**

* Developing high-impact video creative (6s and 15s formats).  
* Ensuring ads are optimized for audibility (98% sound-on benchmark) and viewability.  
* Incorporating "Sitelinks for Action" to drive higher clicks and lower CPAs (e.g., "Make an Appointment," "Upfront Pricing").

## **Optimization & Reporting**

* Continuous monitoring of creative performance with a focus on pausing underperforming assets (e.g., those with CPAs significantly higher than the campaign average).  
* Detailed brand measurement reports covering awareness, ad recall, and consideration lift.
