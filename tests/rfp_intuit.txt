Request for Proposal: Intuit Digital Marketing & Acquisition Campaign

1. Executive Summary
Intuit Inc. is seeking a strategic marketing partner for an integrated campaign during the upcoming tax and small business financial season to drive brand consideration and acquire new users for our flagship products, TurboTax and QuickBooks.

2. Company Background & Context
Intuit operates in a highly seasonal consumer tax preparation market (peak Jan-April for TurboTax) alongside year-round small business accounting and payroll needs (QuickBooks), requiring clear, authoritative, and empathetic brand messaging to help consumers and small business owners navigate complex financial landscapes.

3. Campaign Objectives
- Increase Brand Consideration (relative lift >= 2.2%)
- Maximize Reach (unique reach 11M+ users)
- Drive Efficiency (CPLU ~$0.81, 24% lower CPA for specialized small business/multicultural segments)
- Search Lift (increase brand searches, benchmark: 69%)

4. Target Audience
- Broad consumer segments seeking simplified tax filing and small business owners seeking accounting/payroll ease.
- Key Demographics: Age 35-44 and Working Class Savers (highly responsive segments).
- Specialized small business and multicultural segments (USM).
- Mobile-first users (91% on-target reach).

5. Scope of Work & Deliverables
- Strategic Media Planning (YouTube multi-funnel, tCPA bidding, Custom Intent segments)
- Creative Production (Concise 6s and 15s video creative, optimized audibility, Sitelinks for Action)
- Campaign Optimization & Brand Lift Reporting

6. Submission Requirements
Please provide a proposal detailing:
- Agency Profile (experience in seasonal retail, financial services, and SaaS)
- Strategic Approach (methodology for target consumer and SMB segments)
- Media Plan (channels and mobile reach)
- Measurement Plan (proposed KPIs and methodology)
- Budget (spend allocation, ~$100k initial flights)
