Request for Proposal: National Women's Soccer League (NWSL) Digital Marketing & Fan Growth Campaign

1. Executive Summary
The National Women's Soccer League (NWSL) is seeking a strategic agency partner to design and execute an integrated digital advertising campaign for the upcoming season. Our primary objectives are to accelerate league brand awareness, drive stadium match attendance, increase broadcast/streaming viewership (CBS, ESPN, Prime, Ion), and acquire new, highly engaged sports fans.

2. Context & Business Volatility
The NWSL season operates from March to November. Marketing spend is highly seasonal, concentrated around key tentpoles: Season Opener (March), Summer Olympic/Tournament flights, and the Championship Playoffs (October/November). Messaging must drive emotional connection to the athletes and clubs, translating interest into active match attendance and TV tune-in.

3. Campaign Objectives & Performance Benchmarks
- Increase Brand Consideration & Fan Base (aim for relative lift >= 3.0%)
- Maximize Digital Reach (target unique reach of 15M+ sports and soccer fans)
- Drive Ticket Sales & Broadcast Viewership (Lower Cost Per Acquisition for season tickets, single-match tickets, and TV streaming tune-in)
- Search Lift (drive brand and club keyword search volume, benchmark: +70%)

4. Target Audience
- "The Passionate Supporter": Core soccer/sports fans seeking high-intensity match day engagement.
- "The Family Outing": Families seeking accessible, exciting local sporting events.
- Key Demographics: Gen Z and Millennials, particularly female sports fans and families.
- Mobile-First & Streaming-First Users (91% of audience consumes highlights and matches on mobile/tablet or Connected TV).

5. Scope of Work & Deliverables
- Strategic Planning (Connected TV / CTV planning, YouTube multi-funnel, tCPA bidding for ticket sales, Custom Intent segments targeting adjacent sports leagues)
- High-Impact Creative (Highly audibility-optimized 6s and 15s video, vertical formats for social platforms, "Sitelinks for Action" driving ticket purchases and streaming links)
- Campaign Optimization & Brand Lift measurement (continuous creative testing, rapid scaling of top-performing highlights)

6. Submission Requirements
Please provide a proposal detailing:
- Agency Profile (experience with sports, entertainment, or rapid-scale event marketing)
- Strategic Approach (methodology for growing fan engagement and TV tune-in)
- Media Plan (channel mix optimized for mobile, CTV, and social)
- Measurement Plan (proposed KPIs and brand lift studies)
- Budget (proposed spend allocation, starting with initial flights)
