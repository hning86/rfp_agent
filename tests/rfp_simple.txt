Request for Proposal: H&R Block Advertising Campaign

1. Executive Summary
H&R Block is seeking an advertising partner for an integrated campaign during the upcoming tax season to drive brand consideration and reach the "Sensible Optimist" target audience.

2. Company Background & Context
H&R Block operates in a highly seasonal tax preparation market (peak Jan-April), requiring clear, authoritative messaging to help consumers navigate seasonal complexities.

3. Campaign Objectives
- Increase Brand Consideration (relative lift >= 2.2%)
- Maximize Reach (unique reach 11M+ users)
- Drive Efficiency (CPLU ~$0.81, 24% lower CPA for multicultural)
- Search Lift (increase brand searches, benchmark: 69%)

4. Target Audience
- Broad "Sensible Optimist" audience
- Age group 35-44 and Working Class Savers
- Multicultural (USM) online segments
- Mobile-first users (91% on-target reach)

5. Scope of Work & Deliverables
- Strategic Media Planning (YouTube multi-funnel, tCPA bidding, Custom Intent segments)
- Creative Production (Concise 6s and 15s video creative, optimized audibility, Sitelinks for Action)
- Campaign Optimization & Brand Lift Reporting

6. Submission Requirements
Please provide a proposal detailing:
- Agency Profile (experience in seasonal retail and finance)
- Strategic Approach (methodology for Sensible Optimists)
- Media Plan (channels and mobile reach)
- Measurement Plan (proposed KPIs and methodology)
- Budget (spend allocation, ~$100k initial flights)
